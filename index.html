The Journey

Three phases to your wings

We've structured the path so it fits around your career — not the other way around.

01

Theory

Aviation fundamentals, meteorology, navigation, and flight regulations — taught in evening sessions by active pilots who speak business, not jargon.

  • Self-paced online modules
  • Weekly evening workshops
  • Practice exams included
02

Simulator

Build muscle memory and confidence in our full-motion flight simulators. Make every real-world flight hour count by arriving prepared.

  • Full-motion Cessna 172 sim
  • Emergency scenario training
  • Instrument familiarity
03

Flying

Take the controls. Dual instruction building to your first solo flight and beyond — all the way to your Recreational Pilot Certificate.

  • Dual instruction flights
  • First solo milestone
  • Certification flight test
